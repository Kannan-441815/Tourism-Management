When to visit Kashmir?

Kashmir is worth visiting any time of the year, but it is especially pleasant during spring and winter. Choose a time depending on your budget and interests. From March to May, the spring season is popular among tourists looking to soak in the valley’s beauty as it comes alive because of the fruits and flowers that bloom during this time of the year. Thanks to the snow-clad mountain peaks, lush meadows, pleasant weather, gushing streams, lakes, and the blooming gardens and valleys, every corner in Kashmir looks straight out of a postcard. We offer Kashmir honeymoon packages that help you experience Kashmir at its spring best. But if you want to cosy up against a warm fireplace amidst the backdrop of snow-clad peaks and frozen lakes, or are a skiing enthusiast, November to January is the best time to visit Kashmir.You could also take a gondola cable car ride to enjoy the white carpeted valleys.

When to visit Kashmir?

The Solang Valley has located 13 km from Manali and is famous for its glaciers and snow-capped mountains. During the winter, people visit the Valley to enjoy skiing. And in summer, the place is famous for zorbing and paragliding. Some of the other things you can do at Solang Valley include camping, quad biking, and snowmobiling.
